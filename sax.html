Sax · Case Study

Sax is a song by Fleur East whose lyrics were drawn for the Kinetic Type project. The colours were influenced by the original music video, as well as the transitions. The font choices are based off of the single’s cover art. The goal of this assignment was to understand the Null Object’s many uses and to stay true to the artist’ final vision.
