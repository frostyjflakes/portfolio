Sax · Case Study

Sax is the title of the song by Fleur East and the lyrics for the Kinetic Type project. The colours were drawn from the original music video, as well as the transitions. The font choices are based off of the single’s cover art. The goal of this assignment was to understand the Null Object’s many uses and stay true to the artist’ final vision.
