About · Jennifer Sommerfeld

Bonjour! My name is Jennifer and I’m a bilingual Graphic Designer based in Ottawa, Ontario. I specialize in branding, motion graphics and illustration. I love to experiment with different mediums and explore other creative avenues.

It all started when I asked my mother if she could draw an elephant for me. At the time, I couldn't appreciate the effort that my mom put into her drawing, so I cried because of it. She handed me the pencil and that's when I discovered my passion in life.

As a creative, my goal is to turn people’s ideas into personalised designs. To tell their story through my work. If you’re interested in working together, click on the paper airplane at the bottom of the page.
