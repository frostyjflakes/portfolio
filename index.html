About · Jennifer Sommerfeld

Bonjour! My name is Jen and I'm a Graphic Designer based in Ottawa, ON. I specialize in branding, motion graphics and illustration but I'm capable of creating all sorts of print and digital designs.

It all started when I was 5 years old. I asked my mom if she could draw an elephant for me. Everything was going as planned, until she showed me her work. I started to cry so she handed me the pencil and that's when I discovered my passion in life.

As a designer, I want to tell a story through my work and inspire others to try. Shoot me an email at jennifer@sommerfeldesigns.ca or click on the envelope below!
