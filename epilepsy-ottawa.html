Epilepsy Ottawa · Case Study

A not-for-profit centre that supports people who are affected by epilepsy and seizure disorders. I was brought on, in order to help spread awareness through print and digital products. I was tasked with creating the annual report for the year 2018, advertising community events, as well as other design updates around the offices.
