Vintage · Case Study

Vintage is the theme for the 5 second video challenge. It’s defined as the time that something of quality was produced and the object of a clock is a perfect example of this. As for the colour scheme, the non-traditional retro palette is derived from the design agency, Character Creative and their Bicycle Brewery branding. There’s five clocks for each second of the video.
