Chamber · Internship

As a soon to be college graduate, I believe the time I have spent at the Canadian Chamber of Commerce is valuable to me. More specifically my future as a Graphic Designer.

After my interview, members of the chamber offered to take me on a tour of their floor. I remember thinking that if chosen, I was in good hands. The decor was clearly inspired by their brand. It gave me a sense of community and professionalism. I really appreciated having my own space. I felt like they trusted me to maintain the chamber’s image with a fair amount of projects to work on. Despite having spent most hours working independently, the Graphics team was always there to lend a hand.

Overall, my experience at the Canadian Chamber of Commerce was a positive one. I learnt so much working alongside their team of professionals. I am grateful for the opportunity and I cannot wait to see what the future holds.
