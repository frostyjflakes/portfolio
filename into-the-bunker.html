Into The Bunker · Case Study

Into The Bunker is the title of the Parallax project and the name of episode 2, season 2 of the television series, Gravity Falls. The story’s main characters; Dipper, Mabel, Wendy and Soos, come across a hidden bunker which is rumoured to have belonged to the author of the mysterious journals. The scenery descends into the bunker.
