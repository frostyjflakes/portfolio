Into The Bunker · Case Study

Into The Bunker is the title of the Parallax project and episode 2, season 2 of the Gravity Falls series. The story’s main characters, Dipper, Mabel, Wendy and Soos, come across a hidden bunker which is rumoured to have belonged to the author of the mysterious journals. The goal of this assignment was to understand the proper use of the Camera and Null Object. The scenery descends into the bunker.
