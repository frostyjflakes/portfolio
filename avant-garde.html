Avant-Garde · Case Study

Avant-Garde is a fictitious art supplies store who’s competing against other big name brands, like DeSerres. This project was initially a branding assignment that was suggested by the design team, at k6 Media. The logo was inspired by the tools that are most often used in the visual arts and design scene, such as paint swatches.
